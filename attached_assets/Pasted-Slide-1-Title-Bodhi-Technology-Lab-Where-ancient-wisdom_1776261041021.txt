Slide 8: AI Agent Deep Dive
How agents are configured per temple:
1. Temple selects doctrinal mode: Zen, Pure Land, Theravāda, Vajrayāna, General Buddhist
2. Temple chooses response style: Compassionate, Formal, Balanced
3. Temple uploads own teachings via Google Drive (sutras, dharma talks, instructions)
4. Temple can add custom AI notes (specific instructions, topics to address or avoid)
Built-in guardrails:
- Each agent has scoped system prompt defining what it will/won't do
- Agents defer to human teachers for deep questions
- Terms of service: "AI agents are tools for guidance, not substitutes for qualified teachers"
- Right Speech moderation for community forums
- Ethical safeguards: no medical advice, no definitive claims about enlightenment
4 Doctrinal Vehicles:
- Tiểu Thừa (Foundation): basic mindfulness, Five Precepts, stress reduction
- Trung Thừa (Insight): vipassana, self-inquiry, dependent origination
- Đại Thừa (Bodhisattva): compassion + wisdom, direct pointing, verse-based awakening
- Phật Thừa (Ultimate): sudden awakening, non-conceptual, beyond all teachings
